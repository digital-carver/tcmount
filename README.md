tcmount - mount or unmount a TrueCrypt volume

Usage: $0 [m|u] <mapmountname> [path/to/file/or/device]
m - mounts a volume container file/device
u - unmounts a volume
mapmountname will be used to mkdirs in /media/`whoami` and /dev/mapper
